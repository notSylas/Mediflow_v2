## Vision

### 10x Check

The booking funnel is solved (DB-enforced no-double-booking, pay-at-booking).
The 10x lever left on the table is the doctor's *clinical* time and the
patient's *channel*: a voice-dictation scratchpad would cut SOAP-typing time,
and WhatsApp delivery (not email) matches where this patient population
(Razorpay/Asia-Kolkata/OTP stack implies India) actually lives. Two-x effort
for a meaningfully more-than-two-x improvement in doctor time saved and
patient reachability.

### Platonic Ideal

Doctor opens one tab all day: `/doctor/appointments`. Patient books, pays,
gets a WhatsApp message that feels personal. Doctor takes the call, prescribes,
patient gets a forwardable Rx a pharmacy can act on with no login. Doctor gets
one weekly email: bookings, revenue, no-show rate — visibility without a
dashboard (PRD explicitly cut dashboards; a passive digest respects that cut).
